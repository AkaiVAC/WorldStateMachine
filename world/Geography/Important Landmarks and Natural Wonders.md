# Important Landmarks and Natural Wonders

Excelsia contains numerous landmarks of geographical, historical, or magical significance. The Sun Pillar in Sunnaria's capital serves as the continent's official timekeeping standard. The Silver Falls in Limaria provide both breathtaking beauty and hydro-power for forges and mills. The Star-Spire Observatory in Ilaria represents the continent's premier astronomical research facility. The Great Library of Regala houses Lindward's accumulated knowledge and rare texts. The Frozen Citadel in Stuttgart stands as an engineering marvel built into a glacier. The Moon-Pool in Lunaria's capital holds religious significance and enhances lunar magic. The Heartwood in Aeldrin's forest contains the oldest living trees and serves as spiritual center for wood elves. The Forge-Falls of Ironforge combine natural waterfall power with dwarven engineering for massive metalworking. The Spice-Mesa in Zyronia produces unique seasonings found nowhere else. These landmarks often serve multiple purposes: practical utility, spiritual significance, tourist attractions, and symbols of national pride or technological achievement.
