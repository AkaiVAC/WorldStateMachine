# Capital Cities - Detailed Entries

Each kingdom's capital serves as political, economic, and cultural heart. Sunndor in Sunnaria rises from luminous sandstone along the Golden Delta, its architecture blending practical trade facilities with elegant palaces. Silvercrest in Limaria combines formidable fortifications with renowned warrior academies and armories. Regala in Lindward displays breathtaking opulence with marble palaces, jeweled temples, and luxurious merchant quarters. Stuttgart's capital Frostheim embodies northern resilience with stone fortresses built into mountain sides and heated underground districts. Iluminara in Ilaria features impossible elven architecture with towers growing from living trees and magical light systems. Nocturnia in Lunaria showcases moonstone towers and shadowy elegance reflecting its matriarchal mystery. Aeldrin's capital Silverwood Grove integrates completely with the ancient forest, its structures woven from living trees. Ironforge's great hall Thunderpeak represents dwarven mastery of stone, with precisely carved pillars, forges, and gem-lit chambers. Zyronia's tribal capital Jungle-Heart blends natural materials with vibrant colors in its communal structures.
