# Key Exports and Imports by Kingdom

Each kingdom specializes in specific exports while depending on imports for essential goods. Sunnaria exports river transport services, grain surpluses, and diplomatic mediation. Lunaria exports moonsilver, naval expertise, art, and jewelry. Ilaria exports magical artifacts, elven-crafted goods, and religious texts. Limaria exports mercenary contracts, weapons, armor, and military training. Stuttgart exports minerals, metals, cold-weather gear, and heavy infantry. Lindward exports luxury goods, pearls, gemstones, and financial services. Aeldrin exports timber, elven crafts, magical herbs, and fine wines. Ironforge exports precious metals, gemstones, dwarven weapons, and armor. Zyronia exports exotic spices, rare pelts, tropical fruits, and magical artifacts. Critical dependencies include Stuttgart's need for food imports, Limaria's reliance on luxury goods for noble consumption, and all kingdoms' dependence on Ironforge's mountain passes for overland trade.
