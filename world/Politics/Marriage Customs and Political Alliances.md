# Marriage Customs and Political Alliances

Marriage serves both personal and political purposes across Excelsia, with customs varying significantly by kingdom. Royal marriages typically involve extensive negotiations regarding dowries, inheritance rights, and political implications. Sunnaria's diplomatic marriages often involve multiple smaller alliances rather than single major unions. Limaria's warrior culture emphasizes martial compatibility alongside political considerations. Stuttgart's meritocratic approach sometimes sees royals marrying accomplished commoners. Ilaria's elven marriages consider centuries-long political implications and magical bloodlines. Lunaria's matriarchal system means royal husbands typically come from subordinate positions. Lindward's merchant princes often marry for economic advantage and business connections. Cross-kingdom marriages require navigating different legal systems, religious traditions, and cultural expectations. Marriage contracts specify rights, responsibilities, and succession arrangements in meticulous detail. Failed political marriages can create diplomatic crises, while successful ones build enduring alliances. The children of international marriages often serve as cultural bridges but may face challenges regarding their identity and loyalties. Marriage remains one of the most powerful tools for building and maintaining political relationships between kingdoms.
