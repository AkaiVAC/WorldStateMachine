# Queen Hildegard von Eisenwald of Stuttgart

Queen Hildegard von Eisenwald of Stuttgart, thirty-five years old, stands 175 centimeters tall with a sturdy, powerful build reflecting her northern heritage. Her long brown hair, often worn in traditional Stuttgart braids, frames a strong-featured face with piercing blue eyes that assess everything with calculating precision. Born to the powerful Eisenwald noble family that controlled Stuttgart's eastern territories, Hildegard married Reinhardt when he was a rising military commander, her family's support crucial to his eventual conquest of the throne. Intelligent and politically shrewd, Hildegard operates as Reinhardt's partner in rule rather than mere consort. She manages Stuttgart's internal administration while he focuses on military expansion, her organizational skills ensuring the kingdom's rapid growth doesn't collapse into chaos.
