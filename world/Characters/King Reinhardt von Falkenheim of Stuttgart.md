# King Reinhardt von Falkenheim of Stuttgart

King Reinhardt von Falkenheim has ruled Stuttgart for nearly two decades, his commanding presence marked by broad shoulders, graying brown hair, piercing blue eyes, and the weight of a gold crown. Approaching middle age, he united the kingdom after a brutal civil war through brilliant military strategy and ruthless political maneuvering. A former army strategist with grand ambitions of conquest, Reinhardt married into a powerful noble family to secure his throne and expand his alliances. Known for cunning and long-term planning, he constantly schemes for greater power and territorial expansion. Reinhardt's leadership has transformed Stuttgart from a collection of warring northern territories into a unified military power respected and feared throughout Excelsia.
