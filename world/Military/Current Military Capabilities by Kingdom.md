# Current Military Capabilities by Kingdom

Military power varies significantly across Excelsia's kingdoms. Limaria fields the renowned Red Legion of 25,000 professional soldiers plus 15,000 mercenaries under contract, specializing in heavy infantry and cavalry. Stuttgart maintains 30,000 troops optimized for northern warfare, including specialized cold-weather units and siege engineers. Sunnaria relies on a smaller standing army of 12,000 but can rapidly mobilize 40,000 militia through its wealthy population and strategic position. Ilaria combines 20,000 conventional troops with elite magical support from its academies.
