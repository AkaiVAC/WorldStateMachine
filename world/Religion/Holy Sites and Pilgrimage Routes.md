# Holy Sites and Pilgrimage Routes

Major holy sites attract pilgrims from across Excelsia, with established routes facilitating spiritual journeys. The Sun-Pillar in Sunnaria's capital serves as the primary Solarius pilgrimage destination, particularly during equinox ceremonies. Lunara's Silver Pool in the Lunarian capital draws those seeking prophetic dreams and lunar blessings. Ilarien's Great Library in Ilaria represents the ultimate destination for knowledge seekers and truth pilgrims. Valorax's First Battlefield in Limaria hosts warriors seeking honor and strategic wisdom. Mercuros's Crossroads Temple in Sunnaria blesses travelers and merchants beginning new ventures. Silvanna's Heartwood in Aeldrin's forest welcomes those seeking harmony with nature. Forgefather's Master Forge in Ironforge attracts artisans pursuing perfection in their craft. Pilgrimage routes typically follow safe roads with way-temples providing shelter, though some involve challenging journeys through wilderness or dangerous territories. Pilgrimage seasons align with holy days and favorable weather, creating annual patterns of religious travel that also facilitate cultural exchange and economic activity along the routes.
