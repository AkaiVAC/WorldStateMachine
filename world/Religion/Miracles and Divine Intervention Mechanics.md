# Miracles and Divine Intervention Mechanics

Miracles represent direct divine intervention distinct from learned magic, though the boundary often blurs in practice. True miracles typically occur during moments of extreme need, profound faith, or at holy sites during significant religious events. They manifest as healing beyond magical capacity, protection from certain doom, guidance in impossible situations, or manifestations of divine presence. Unlike spellcasting, miracles don't deplete personal mana but may require spiritual preparation, ritual purity, or specific conditions. Different deities manifest distinctively: Solarius provides clarity and protection, Lunara offers visions and mysteries, Ilarien grants wisdom and revelation, Valorax enables impossible victories, Mercuros facilitates crucial meetings, Silvanna enables communion with nature, and Forgefather inspires masterwork creation. Skeptics attribute miracles to unusual magical phenomena or psychological effects, while believers see direct divine action. Temple authorities carefully document and investigate claimed miracles to distinguish genuine divine intervention from magical accidents or fraud. The relationship between faith, magic, and actual divine presence remains one of Excelsia's great theological questions.
